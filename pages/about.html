Originally from sunny California, I moved to the greater Seattle area seeking the vast forests and lakes and immediately fell in love. Before moving to Washington I lived in Oregon and worked at a small web agency where I got to fully realize my passion for design work. Since then I have worked in-house and freelanced for several small businesses as well as for a large non-profit.

As a designer I am extremely empathic, communicative and an active listener. I am always curious about people and how to find ways to best support their needs. I love learning new things and am constantly experimenting or asking questions to figure out how things work or how to improve my workflow. Currently, I am studying User Experience Design at the School of Visual Concepts and am looking to further hone my design chops within a role that can utilize my diverse set of skills.
